Note on license expiration in GitLab 7.10.5 EE

If you’re upgrading to GitLab Enterprise Edition 7.11, which introduces licenses keys, you’re probably planning to upgrade to 7.10.5 first. This way you are able to upload your license key in advance.

One of our customers notified us of a faulty description in the license uploader in GitLab 7.10.5. Upon uploading, the license is checked properly, however the text in the license view in the admin page in GitLab will show:
