Help Us Improve JetBrains.com and Win a License

From:http://blog.jetbrains.com/blog/2015/06/01/help-us-improve-jetbrains-com-and-win-a-license/


It has been a while since the last time that we updated our web site design, nearly three years ago when we switched to the current design from the one below.

We’re thinking about making another update some time soon, but as a team of very technical geeks we love numbers. We do have lots of data already from the different analytics systems we’re using but we want to do a special survey right now dedicated specifically for JetBrains.com.

It is people like you who are visiting the web site and using it to find the information that you need, so we are asking for your help.

As the survey is about a web site, it might feel odd that we’re asking some questions which might not seem relevant. However, often decisions we make are somewhat related to other aspects in our lives. We’re catering the site to so many diverse individuals and some of the questions play an role in this. Having said that, some questions are optional.

If you are willing to help us, please complete our survey.

And yes, we have some prizes for those who complete the survey: a chance to win one of 10 personal licenses for a JetBrains product of your choice, or one of 20 Amazon vouchers worth $25.

Thank you!
